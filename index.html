The Reality

How climate change
affects our world today

From record-breaking heatwaves to vanishing coastlines, the effects of our warming planet are already here — and they're accelerating.

🌡️

Extreme Heat

Deadly heatwaves are now five times more likely than in 1950. By 2050, billions of people could face life-threatening heat conditions for weeks each year without rapid action.

🌊

Rising Oceans

Sea levels have risen 20 cm since 1900 and are accelerating. Over 1 billion people live in low-elevation coastal zones threatened by flooding and storm surges.

🌳

Biodiversity Loss

One million species face extinction due to climate change and habitat destruction. Forests that once absorbed carbon are now releasing it as wildfires intensify.

🌾

Food & Water Crisis

Shifting rainfall patterns and droughts threaten agricultural systems worldwide. Up to 216 million people could be displaced by 2050 due to climate impacts.

Reasons for Hope

The solutions exist.
We just need to scale them.

Renewable energy is now cheaper than coal. Electric vehicles are outselling expectations. Nature-based solutions are storing carbon at scale. Progress is real.

☀️

Solar & Wind Revolution

Solar energy costs fell 89% in a decade. Renewables now supply over 30% of global electricity. The clean energy transition is faster than any technology adoption in history.

🌿

Nature-Based Solutions

Protecting and restoring forests, wetlands and oceans could provide up to 30% of the emissions reductions we need by 2030 while supporting biodiversity.

⚡

Clean Transportation

Electric vehicle sales hit record highs worldwide. 14 countries have pledged to phase out petrol cars by 2035. Public transit investment is surging globally.

🌍

Global Momentum

Over 140 countries have committed to net-zero targets. Youth movements, climate litigation, and ESG investing are reshaping political and financial systems.
